I possess the unique ability to create visually impactful designs that are strategically aligned with driving retail performance, and I am keen to contribute those skills to a company whose accessible, high-energy visuals resonate with such a broad, diverse audience. This role offers the perfect opportunity to apply my strategic marketing knowledge and creative graphic design skills to help evolve and execute campaigns that continue to solidify Old Navy's welcoming and vibrant presence in the retail industry.

My Graphic Design skills are tailored to creating the kind of clean, impactful, and authentic visual storytelling required to communicate complex sustainability initiatives and engage a highly conscious customer base. Paired with my Marketing background, I can ensure that every creative asset—from digital campaigns to printed materials—is not only aesthetically powerful but also strategically supports the company's core mission and business goals, driving both brand loyalty and positive change.
